Rabdi


Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pour the milk into a heavy-bottomed pan and bring it to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Once the milk comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  3. After about 10-15 minutes of simmering, the milk will start to thicken. Keep scraping the cream that forms on the sides of the pan and mix it back into the milk.
  4. Continue simmering the milk until it reduces to about half of its original quantity and becomes thick and creamy. This will take around 1 to 1.5 hours.
  5. Once the milk has thickened, add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron strands. Mix well until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  6. Let the rabdi simmer for another 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Turn off the heat and let the rabdi cool down to room temperature.
  8. Once cooled, transfer the rabdi to a serving bowl and garnish with chopped nuts.
  9. Chill the rabdi in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving.
  10. Serve the chilled rabdi as a delightful dessert!

Lassi


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup chilled water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)
  • A few saffron strands for garnish (optional)
  • Crushed ice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add plain yogurt, chilled water, sugar, salt, and ground cardamom (if using).
  2. Blend the mixture until smooth and frothy.
  3. If desired, add crushed ice to the blender and blend again until the lassi is chilled and frothy.
  4. Pour the lassi into serving glasses.
  5. Garnish each glass with a few saffron strands for an extra touch of flavor and aroma.
  6. Serve the refreshing lassi immediately.
  7. You can also refrigerate the lassi for a few hours before serving if you prefer it chilled.
  8. Enjoy your homemade lassi as a cooling drink on a hot day or with your favorite meal!

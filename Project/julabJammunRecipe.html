Julab Jammun


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or melted unsalted butter
  • 3-4 tablespoons milk (as needed to knead the dough)
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • For the Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • Rose water or kewra water (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking powder.
  2. Add ghee or melted unsalted butter to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  3. Gradually add milk, a little at a time, and knead the mixture into a soft and smooth dough. The dough should be soft but not sticky. Add more milk if needed.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes.
  5. After resting, knead the dough again for a few minutes to make it smooth.
  6. Divide the dough into equal-sized small balls and roll each ball between your palms to make smooth, crack-free balls.
  7. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  8. Once the oil is hot, reduce the heat to low and gently slide the rolled dough balls into the hot oil.
  9. Fry the dough balls on low heat, stirring occasionally, until they turn golden brown and evenly cooked from all sides.
  10. Remove the fried dough balls from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  11. Prepare the sugar syrup by combining sugar, water, cardamom powder, saffron strands (if using), and rose water or kewra water (if using) in a saucepan.
  12. Bring the sugar syrup to a boil and simmer for 5-7 minutes until it slightly thickens.
  13. Turn off the heat and add the fried dough balls (gulab jamuns) to the hot sugar syrup.
  14. Let the gulab jamuns soak in the sugar syrup for at least 1-2 hours before serving, allowing them to absorb the flavors and become soft and juicy.
  15. Serve the delicious Gulab Jamuns warm or at room temperature, garnished with some chopped nuts or dried rose petals if desired.

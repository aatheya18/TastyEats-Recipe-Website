Pudding


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional toppings: whipped cream, fresh berries, chocolate shavings

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the whole milk over medium heat until it starts to steam but is not boiling.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  3. Whisk the eggs into the dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Gradually pour the hot milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent curdling.
  5. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the pudding thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  7. Transfer the pudding to individual serving dishes or a large serving bowl.
  8. Cover the surface of the pudding with plastic wrap to prevent a skin from forming, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until chilled and set.
  9. Once chilled, serve the pudding topped with whipped cream, fresh berries, or chocolate shavings if desired.
  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade pudding!
